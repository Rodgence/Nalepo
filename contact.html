Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organization and programs.

How can I volunteer with NALEPO?

We welcome volunteers with various skills and backgrounds. Please fill out our contact form with "Volunteer Opportunities" as the subject, and we'll get back to you with current opportunities that match your interests and skills.

How are donations used?

Your donations go directly to our programs: 45% to program implementation, 25% to staffing, 15% to administration, 10% to monitoring & evaluation, and 5% to contingency funds. We provide regular reports on fund utilization.

Can I visit your projects in person?

Yes! We organize field visits for donors, partners, and volunteers. Please contact us in advance to arrange a visit. We'll coordinate with our field teams to ensure you get a comprehensive view of our work.

How do you measure impact?

We use rigorous monitoring and evaluation systems, tracking KPIs like number of trees planted, income growth, literacy rates, and number of trainees. We conduct quarterly field assessments and maintain continuous feedback loops with communities.
