Success Stories

Real stories from community members whose lives have been transformed.

Nasirian Sankale

Maasai Mother & Entrepreneur

"Through NALEPO's micro-loan program, I started a beadwork business. Now I can send all my children to school and support other women in my community. The training gave me confidence to become a leader."
Impact: Started business, educated 4 children, mentors 10 women

Joseph Lemayian

Vocational Training Graduate

"I learned carpentry skills through NALEPO's vocational program. Today, I run a successful furniture workshop and employ 5 young people from my village. Education changed everything for my family."
Impact: Established workshop, created 5 jobs, increased income by 300%

Mary Parsitau

Environmental Champion

"Our community was struggling with deforestation. NALEPO taught us sustainable farming and helped us plant 300 trees. Now our land is green again, and our crops are thriving."
Impact: Led reforestation of 10 hectares, improved crop yields by 40%
